What are Consoles?

Video game consoles are dedicated machines that are made to play games when plugged into a television or monitor. Unlike PCs, they are design for gaming to be in mind. Which makes it simple, popular. From the early 2000s until now, they have become a big part of millions of peoples lives. The consoles have not only just brought new ways for people to play, but for innovations in the 2000s and the 2010s.

Why are Consoles Important in Gaming History

Video game consoles are important in gaming history because it has gave the opportinity for millions of people to play games, making it something as a cultural experience. Instead of requiring an expensive computer to run such games, consoles have made it much simple as just turning on your console, your controller, monitor (or tv), click a game and start having fun. Over time, these consoles have introduced cool technologies such as playing online, which has made it easier for people to connect through them. Consoles of itself, became one of the reasons of why gaming became a global experience.

What I Will Cover In This Website

This website will explore the history of modern video game consoles accross two decades. The first decade being the 2000s, which has introduced playing online through systems like Xbox live and motion controls with the Nintendo Wii. The 2010s, which have pushed gaming into a digital era with powerful hardware like the Playstation 4, new online services, and systems like the nintendo switch. These consoles have not just shaped the way we play, but how gaming became a big part of everyones lives around the world.
